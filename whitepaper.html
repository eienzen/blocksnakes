Introduction

BlockSnakes is a blockchain-based 3D snake game where players can play, earn, and stake BST tokens. Built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), BlockSnakes combines the classic snake game with Web3 technology to create a play-to-earn (P2E) ecosystem.

Tokenomics

The BST token is the core of the BlockSnakes ecosystem. Players earn 10 BST tokens for completing each level. They can stake these tokens to earn rewards or unstake them anytime. Total supply: 1,000,000 BST. Token launch date: 1st May 2025.

Game Mechanics

Players control a snake in a 3D environment, collecting food to grow and earn points. Reach a score of 100 to complete a level and earn 10 BST tokens. Use the "Next Level" button to increase difficulty and continue earning rewards.

Roadmap

- Q1 2025: Testnet deployment and initial testing.
- Q2 2025: Mainnet launch and token sale on 1st May 2025.
- Q3 2025: Listing on PancakeSwap and adding multiplayer mode.
- Q4 2025: NFT integration for unique snake skins.
